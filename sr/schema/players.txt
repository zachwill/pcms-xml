**Notes:**
- `sr_id` is the SportRadar UUID.
- `reference` is the official NBA.com / Elias player ID for cross-system joins.
- `sr_team_id` is the current team from the profile payload.
- `seasons_json` stores the career statistics summary found in the Player Profile endpoint.
- Height and weight are stored as integers (inches and lbs). SportRadar often provides height in inches.
- `references_json` preserves the full external IDs array (NBA ID, etc.) provided in the `references` field.
