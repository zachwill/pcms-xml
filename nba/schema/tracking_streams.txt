### nba.tracking_streams

- stream_id text PRIMARY KEY
- game_id text REFERENCES nba.games(game_id)
- stream_name text
- processor_name text
- status text -- created, started, processing, finished, timed_out, expired
- stream_created_at timestamptz
- expires_at timestamptz

- created_at timestamptz
- updated_at timestamptz
- fetched_at timestamptz

#### Constraints & Indexes
- INDEX ON (game_id)
- INDEX ON (status)

#### Notes
- `stream_created_at`: Timestamp from the source indicating when the tracking stream was created.
- Sourced from `nba/api/nba-tracking.txt` and `nba/api/nba-tracking-feed.txt`.
- Tracks availability and status of Hawkeye tracking data streams.
- `stream_id` is the unique identifier used to subscribe via SSE.
